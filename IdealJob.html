Position Description

The advertisement above is one looking for a UI designer to work at Electronic Arts (commonly abbreviated as EA). EA is a video game company that publishes both console and mobile games, and are best known for series like FIFA, Mass Effect, and Battlefield. The UI designer is expected to work in a team to design and implement the UI in EA’s various mobile games from the initial concept to the finalised interface. The designer is also expected to seek user feedback and update the UI accordingly.

What makes this position appealing to me?

For me, this job is appealing for a multitude of reasons.

It aligns with both of my interests perfectly. I’ve been interested in how video games are made for a while, and I’ve found that the areas of IT I’m most interested in involve design in some aspect. This job advert is looking for people that are passionate in precisely those subjects, a description that would fit me nicely. While it is not guaranteed that every element of this job will be enjoyable, being able to do a job that I have familiar knowledge in and am interested in would help to ease me into the work environment.

EA is a well-known company that has developed a number of established franchises.There are a lot of potential opportunities that could arise from working at this company. I’d be able to learn from my colleagues and other professionals in the industry and develop a network of referrals for future jobs.

This job complements my current talents and skills, and would allow me to use them more effectively. My specialty is in art and visual design, and I have knowledge of what constitutes intuitive and appealing design, which I can app What excites me the most about this job is the challenge of trying to design an interface that is easy to understand and facilitates the user to make the most out of its features, while also giving them an idea of the application’s identity. I have an eye for detail, and am good at looking at designs from a client’s point of view and spotting potential usability issues. So this job would give me the perfect opportunity to practice and develop my current skill set.

Skills, qualifications and experience required for the position.

Skills

  • Teamwork Skills. The applicant should be able to cooperate with a team, develop good work relationships, be able to share ideas and bring a project from initial concept to final product. This is important when working with programmers or designers to implement your ideas.

  • Communication skills. The applicant is expected to be able to convey their ideas effectively to other team members and through visual mediums like concept art and prototypes, as well as being open to ideas from their team members.

  • Art and Design Skills, particularly in relation to UI/UX. The applicant is expected to have good knowledge of design principles and be able to apply them to their work. Should also be able to produce clean, professional looking work on a consistent basis.

  • Proficiency in digital graphics software and prototyping software like Photoshop and Figma. Has a good understanding of the tools and is able to use them effectively without needing prior training.

  • Knowledge of industry art and design trends. The applicant can spot relevant design patterns and design their UI accordingly.

  • Ability to take feedback from users and effectively apply it to future updates. This is important to ensure that any usability issues that arise post-launch are met quickly.

  • A passion for mobile and simulation games. The applicant is willing to learn more about the genre and is invested in the project.

  • Some technical knowledge and understanding of UI programming (not mandatory but preferred). This helps the designer have a better judgement of what is a feasible or realistic design, since they know how their designs are implemented in code.

Qualifications

  • Art and design portfolio showing the applicants prior work and particularly giving the employer an idea of how they design their projects and what their style is. (This job does not require University or Academic qualification, but it is preferred)

Experience

  • Prior experience working with industry professionals in developing UI/UX for projects from start to finish, as well as post-launch. This shows that the applicant is capable of not only starting a project, but committing to it all the way to the end, which is a quality sought out by prospective employers.

  • Experience making visual and design guides within brand constraints. This is important if other designers and programers will be maintaining the project in your stead, as a design guide ensures that the visual language of the UI remains consistent.
